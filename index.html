Monster Hunter

How To Play!

Utilize the powerful fantasy weapons and your disposal to dispatch the monsters. Weapon damge is randomized with each new monster!

Monsters have incredibly high HP but don't let that scare you! If you drop their hp to 0 you win. But be careful, if you Over-Kill a monster you will ruin the trophy and lose the round.

Slay all the monsters to win.
